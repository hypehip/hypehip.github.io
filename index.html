HypeHip Features

  • Intuitive

    Everyone already knows how to chat with friends, family, coworkers. The exact same way works for getting information and services from online chat bots.

  • Nothing to download

    Nothing to download, nothing to install, nothing to configure, no need to keep upgrading.

  • You own the conversation

    All chat messages are stored in your channel. If you need to review what happened during your conversation with the service bots/agents, you can easily rewind the conversation and see all the messages.
